QICSS (UQAM-INRS)

This laboratory is located on the Université du Québec à Montréal (UQAM) campus and is one of the four branches affiliated to the Quebec Interuniversity Centre for Social Statistics (QICSS). The laboratory opened its doors in 2005 with the collaboration of the Institut national de la recherche scientifique (INRS). The laboratory became the UQAM-INRS branch in 2007.
